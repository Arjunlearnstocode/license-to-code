Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is one of my favourite go-to dinners I can throw together on a whim

With such simple and cheap ingredients, you can feed a family of 5 too easily

Ingredients

  • 1 Small Onion, chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • 3/4 Cup Coconut Milk
  • 2 Chicken Breasts, diced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • Spices Galore

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over med-heat. Saute onions until golden brown.
  2. Stir in minced garlic and spices over 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken, tomato paste, coconut milk.
  4. First bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer for 25 minutes.
  5. Stir in condiments you might like such as lemon juice or cayenne pepper. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice and enjoy the singing praises!

Beef Burritos

Description

Beefy burritos are where the true bulking gains are at!

Very filling and flexible in what you can add, perfect for both adventurous or picky eaters

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Can Black Beans
  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 1 Pack Taco Seasoning
  • 4 Tortillas
  • 1 Cup Cheddar, shredded
  • 2 Jalapenos, diced

Steps

  1. Cook and stiry ground beef until browned and crumbly. Remove to drain excess fats
  2. Return beef back into pan, add black beans, salsa, water and taco seasoning
  3. Bring to a boil and mix well.
  4. Add cheddar cheese and cover to let it simmer until cheese is melted
  5. Add Jalapenos for heat, and roll up burritos with the tortillas.
  6. Top with sour cream or other condiments prior to the roll
